Where My Passion for Technology Began

In 2018, I had the chance to compete in the Robotacon WRO Vietnam, a national robotics competition co-hosted by Samsung and LEGO Education. Although my team only made it to the city-level round, the experience was a turning point. It was the first time I wrote code, built a robot, and saw how technology could solve real-world problems. Later that same year, I earned a B prize in another local tech competition, which gave me more confidence and motivation to keep learning. These early achievements sparked my passion for programming and led me to explore web development and software design, eventually shaping my academic and career path in tech.
