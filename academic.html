MY ACADEMIC JOURNEY

My academic journey began in Toronto, Canada, where I graduated from John Polanyi Collegiate Institute in 2022. During high school, I built a strong foundation in communication, teamwork, and cultural awareness through both classroom learning and extracurricular involvement.

In the fall of 2024, I continued my education at Cascadia College in Bothell, Washington, where I’m currently pursuing an Associate Degree in Computer Science (expected graduation: June 2025). This program has allowed me to explore my interests at the intersection of technology and business, equipping me with skills in programming (Java, Python, HTML), graphic design, and project coordination.

Beyond the classroom, I’ve actively contributed to the campus community. From leading event promotions and creating marketing materials for cultural events, to assisting new student orientation as a bilingual guide, I’ve developed both technical and interpersonal skills. My academic journey has not only deepened my knowledge, but also shaped me into a motivated, collaborative, and globally minded learner.
